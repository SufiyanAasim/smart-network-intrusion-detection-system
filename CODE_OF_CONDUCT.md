# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent; disagree on ideas, not people.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported via the contact in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
Maintainers may remove comments, commits, or contributors that violate
this code.

Adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), v2.1.
